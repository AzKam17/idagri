# Project Overview - IdAgri

## Purpose
IdAgri is an Agricultural Cooperative Management Application designed to manage:
- Planters (farmers who grow crops like cocoa)
- Transporters and Vehicles
- Weighings (product deliveries)
- Credits (loans to planters)
- Payments and Bulletins
- Bank Statements and Transfer Orders
- Various reports and attestations

The app focuses on the complete workflow from product collection to payment processing.

## Current State
The application currently has:
- ✅ Farmer management (general farmer profiles with photos, QR codes)
- ✅ Plantation management (with GPS coordinates and polygon mapping)
- ✅ Employee management
- ✅ Planter management (cocoa farmers with codes)
- ✅ Weighing tracking
- ✅ Credit tracking
- ✅ Payment system
- ✅ Interactive map with Leaflet
